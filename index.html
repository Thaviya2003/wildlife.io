SRI LANKAN WILDLIFE

Sri Lanka, an island nation in the Indian Ocean, boasts a rich history influenced by Indian, Arab, and European cultures. With diverse landscapes, from lush tea plantations to ancient cities, the country is characterized by a multi-ethnic population, with Buddhism as the predominant religion. Overcoming challenges like a civil conflict, Sri Lanka has made economic progress, especially in tourism, agriculture, and services. Known for its tea, gemstones, festivals, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites, the nation offers a unique blend of attractions, wildlife, and cuisine shaped by its cultural heritage.
